First, what is Mazzaella?
Mazzaella is a genus of a species of seaweed. See this article.

Why did I choose the domain name Mazzaella? Because it reminds me of home, the pacific northwest.
Mazzaella splendens is a beautiful red seaweed. When you view it underwater, it shimmers.
